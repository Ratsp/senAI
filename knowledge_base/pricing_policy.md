### 1.2 Starter Tier — $29/user/month

The Starter tier is designed for small teams and individual users exploring AI-powered CRM intelligence. It includes core platform access with basic analytics and email classification capabilities. Starter is ideal for teams of 1–10 users who need essential functionality without advanced compliance or integration features. API access is limited to **100 requests per minute**. Support is provided via email with a 48-hour response SLA. Data is retained for 30 days after processing.

### 1.3 Standard Tier — $79/user/month

The Standard tier is our most popular plan, built for growing teams that need SSO, webhook integrations, and faster support response times. Standard supports **3 to 50 users** and includes a **500 requests per minute** API rate limit. Email support response SLA is **24 hours**. Standard includes up to 5 configured webhooks for event-driven workflows. Data retention is extended to 90 days. Standard tier subscribers receive access to the SenAI analytics dashboard with category breakdown and sentiment trend reporting.

### 1.4 Professional Tier — $199/user/month

The Professional tier is designed for mid-market and compliance-sensitive organizations. It includes priority support with a **4-hour response SLA**, **2,000 API requests per minute**, custom integrations, a sandbox testing environment, and advanced audit logging. Professional supports **10 to 200 users**. Professional tier includes a **99.9% uptime SLA** and is eligible for **HIPAA Business Associate Agreements (BAA)**. Data retention is 1 year. Up to 25 webhooks can be configured.

### 1.5 Enterprise Tier — Custom Pricing

The Enterprise tier is tailored for large organizations requiring dedicated infrastructure, unlimited API access, custom data residency, a dedicated Customer Success Manager, and full compliance support including HIPAA BAA, GDPR DPA, and SOC 2 Type II report access. Enterprise requires a **minimum of 50 seats**. Pricing is negotiated on a per-deal basis by the SenAI Sales Engineering team. Enterprise agreements typically start at **$150/user/month** for annual commitments of 100+ seats but can vary based on scope, custom SLA requirements, and integration complexity. All Enterprise deals require approval from the VP of Sales. To initiate an Enterprise pricing discussion, contact **sales@senai.io**.

## 2. Non-Profit and Educational Discount Program

### 2.1 Non-Profit Discount

SenAI Solutions offers a **30% discount on the Standard tier** for verified non-profit organizations (501(c)(3) in the US, or equivalent charitable status in other jurisdictions). This discount applies to both monthly and annual billing cycles.

**Non-profit pricing after discount:**
- **Monthly:** $79 × 0.70 = **$55.30/user/month**
- **Annual:** $63 × 0.70 = **$44.10/user/month**

**Eligibility requirements:**
1. Organization must provide proof of non-profit status (e.g., IRS determination letter, charity registration document).
2. The account must be registered under the organization's official domain (personal email addresses are not accepted).
3. The discount applies exclusively to the **Standard tier**. Non-profits requiring Professional or Enterprise features must contact sales@senai.io for a custom quote.
4. The discount is verified annually. Organizations must re-submit proof of status within 30 days of their renewal date.

**How to apply:**
- New customers: Select "Non-Profit" during checkout and upload verification documents.
- Existing customers: Email **billing@senai.io** with subject line "Non-Profit Discount Application" and attach proof of status. The discount will be applied within 5 business days and pro-rated for the current billing cycle.

### 2.2 Educational Institution Discount

Educational institutions (accredited K-12 schools, colleges, and universities) receive a **25% discount on Standard or Professional tiers**. Contact **education@senai.io** for details.

## 4. Pro-Rata Billing Rules

### 4.1 Mid-Cycle Upgrades

When a customer upgrades their subscription tier mid-billing cycle, **pro-rata billing** is applied as follows:

1. **Calculate the remaining days** in the current billing cycle from the upgrade date.
2. **Calculate the daily rate difference** between the new tier and the current tier: `(New Tier Monthly Price − Current Tier Monthly Price) / Days in Billing Month`.
3. **Charge the pro-rated difference** for the remaining days: `Daily Rate Difference × Remaining Days`.
4. Starting from the next full billing cycle, the customer is charged the full new tier price.

**Example:** A customer on the Standard tier ($79/month) upgrades to Professional ($199/month) on day 15 of a 30-day billing month.
- Daily rate difference: ($199 − $79) / 30 = $4.00/day
- Remaining days: 15
- Pro-rated charge: $4.00 × 15 = **$60.00** charged immediately
- Next month onward: $199/month

### 4.2 Mid-Cycle Downgrades

When a customer downgrades their subscription tier mid-billing cycle:

1. The downgrade takes effect at the **start of the next billing cycle**. The customer retains access to higher-tier features for the remainder of the current cycle.
2. **No partial refund is issued** for the unused portion of the higher tier in the current cycle.
3. If the customer has already paid for an annual commitment, the downgrade applies at the next annual renewal date. Early termination of an annual plan to downgrade is subject to the cancellation policy (see Section 8).

### 4.3 Mid-Cycle Seat Changes

- **Adding seats:** Pro-rated charges apply for the remaining days in the current billing cycle, calculated using the same formula as tier upgrades.
- **Removing seats:** Seat reductions take effect at the start of the next billing cycle. No pro-rated refund is issued for unused seats in the current cycle.

## 5. Enterprise Custom Pricing

### 5.1 How Enterprise Pricing Works

Enterprise pricing is negotiated on a case-by-case basis and takes into account:
- Number of seats (minimum 50)
- Custom SLA requirements (e.g., 99.95% uptime, dedicated infrastructure)
- Data residency requirements (US, EU, or APAC regions)
- Integration complexity (custom API integrations, SSO configuration, legacy system connectors)
- Compliance requirements (HIPAA BAA, SOC 2, custom DPAs)
- Contract duration (1-year, 2-year, or 3-year commitments)

### 5.2 Enterprise Pricing Process

1. **Initial Contact:** Customer contacts sales@senai.io or fills out the Enterprise inquiry form on senai.io/enterprise.
2. **Discovery Call:** A Sales Engineer is assigned within 2 business days to conduct a requirements assessment.
3. **Proposal:** A formal pricing proposal is delivered within 5 business days of the discovery call.
4. **Negotiation:** All Enterprise pricing must be approved by the VP of Sales. Discounts exceeding 25% of list price require CFO approval.
5. **Contracting:** Enterprise agreements are formalized as Master Service Agreements (MSAs) with attached Order Forms.

### 5.3 Enterprise Minimum Commitments

- Minimum seat count: **50 seats**
- Minimum annual contract value: **$90,000/year**
- Minimum contract duration: **12 months**

### 5.4 Volume Discounts for Enterprise

| Seat Count   | Typical Discount Range |

| 50–99 seats  | 5–10% off list price   |
| 100–249 seats| 10–15% off list price  |

Volume discounts are applied to the per-user rate and are negotiated during the Enterprise sales process.

## 6. Overage Charges

### 6.1 API Call Overages

If a customer exceeds their tier's API rate limit, the following overage policy applies:

| Tier          | Rate Limit       | Overage Rate            | Grace Period  |
|---------------|------------------|-------------------------|---------------|
| Starter       | 100 req/min      | $0.005 per excess request | 50 requests  |
| Standard      | 500 req/min      | $0.003 per excess request | 100 requests |
| Professional  | 2,000 req/min    | $0.002 per excess request | 200 requests |
| Enterprise    | Custom / Unlimited| Per agreement            | N/A           |

- The **grace period** allows a buffer of requests above the limit before overage charges begin. This prevents billing for occasional, minor spikes.
- Overage charges are calculated per calendar month and added to the next invoice.
- Customers are notified via email when they reach **80%** and **100%** of their rate limit in a given billing period.
- Sustained overages (exceeding the rate limit for 3 or more consecutive months) will trigger a recommendation to upgrade tiers.

### 6.2 Storage Overages

Each tier includes a base storage allocation:
- Starter: 5 GB
- Standard: 25 GB
- Professional: 100 GB
- Enterprise: Custom

Storage overages are billed at **$0.10 per GB per month** for amounts exceeding the tier allocation.

## 7. Trial Period Policy

### 7.1 Free Trial Terms

All new customers are eligible for a **14-day free trial** of the Professional tier. The trial includes full access to Professional features, including API access (rate-limited to 500 req/min during trial), analytics, and the sandbox environment.

**Trial terms:**
1. No credit card is required to start the trial.
2. At the end of the 14-day trial, the account is automatically downgraded to the Starter tier unless the customer selects and pays for a subscription.
3. Data created during the trial is retained for **30 days** after the trial ends. If the customer does not subscribe within 30 days, trial data is permanently deleted.
4. Each organization is limited to **one free trial**. Trial extensions of up to 7 additional days may be granted by a Sales Manager upon request.
5. Trial accounts are not eligible for SLA credits or uptime guarantees.

### 7.2 Extended Trials for Enterprise Prospects

Enterprise prospects evaluating SenAI for large deployments (50+ seats) may request an extended **30-day Proof of Concept (PoC)** trial. PoC trials include dedicated onboarding support from a Solutions Engineer and are coordinated through **sales@senai.io**.

## 8. Payment Terms and Accepted Methods

### 8.1 Payment Methods

SenAI Solutions accepts the following payment methods:

- **Credit/Debit Cards:** Visa, Mastercard, American Express (processed via Stripe)
- **ACH Bank Transfer:** Available for annual subscriptions of $5,000+/year
- **Wire Transfer:** Available for Enterprise contracts; net-30 payment terms apply
- **Invoice (Net-30):** Available for Enterprise customers upon credit approval

### 8.2 Payment Terms

- **Monthly subscriptions:** Payment is due on the billing date (1st of each month or the date of initial subscription). Failed payments trigger a 7-day grace period, followed by account suspension.
- **Annual subscriptions:** Payment is due upfront for the full 12-month term. Annual invoices are issued 30 days before the renewal date.
- **Enterprise (Net-30):** Payment is due within 30 days of invoice date. Late payments incur a **1.5% monthly interest charge** on outstanding balances.

### 8.3 Currency

All pricing is in **US Dollars (USD)**. Customers paying in other currencies are charged at the prevailing exchange rate on the date of the transaction. SenAI does not absorb currency conversion fees.

## 9. Price Lock Guarantee

Customers on **annual subscriptions** receive a price lock guarantee for the duration of their current term:

1. The per-user rate agreed upon at the time of signing will **not increase** during the 12-month term, even if SenAI adjusts its published list prices.
2. Upon renewal, the customer will be quoted the then-current list price. However, customers who renew within **30 days of their renewal date** are eligible for a **loyalty discount of 5%** off the new list price.
3. Multi-year Enterprise agreements (2+ years) include price lock for the entire contract duration.
4. The price lock applies to base subscription fees only. Overage charges, add-on features, and professional services are billed at the rates in effect at the time of usage.

## 10. Cancellation Policy

### 10.1 Monthly Subscriptions

Monthly subscribers may cancel at any time. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle. No prorated refunds are issued for partial months.

### 10.2 Annual Subscriptions

Annual subscribers who cancel before the end of their 12-month term are subject to an **early termination fee** equal to **25% of the remaining contract value**. The early termination fee may be waived at the discretion of the VP of Sales for customers who demonstrate a qualifying hardship.

### 10.3 Enterprise Agreements

Enterprise cancellations are governed by the terms of the individual Master Service Agreement (MSA). Standard enterprise MSAs include a 90-day written notice period for non-renewal.

## 11. Internal Reference: Pricing Scenarios

This section provides worked examples for common pricing scenarios encountered by the support and billing teams.

### Scenario A: Non-Profit Upgrade

A non-profit organization on the Standard tier with 10 users and the 30% NPO discount wants to add 5 more seats mid-cycle (day 10 of 30).
- Current rate per user: $55.30/month (after 30% NPO discount on $79)
- Pro-rated charge per new seat: ($55.30 / 30) × 20 = **$36.87/seat**
- Total pro-rated charge for 5 seats: $36.87 × 5 = **$184.33**
- Next month onward: 15 seats × $55.30 = **$829.50/month**

### Scenario B: Annual Subscriber Renewal with Price Increase

SenAI raises the Standard tier price from $79 to $89/month. A customer renewing their annual Standard subscription within 30 days of their renewal date receives:
- New annual price: $89 × 0.80 (annual discount) = $71.20/month
- Loyalty discount: $71.20 × 0.95 = **$67.64/user/month**
- Annual total per user: $67.64 × 12 = **$811.68/user/year**
